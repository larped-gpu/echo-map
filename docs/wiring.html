Mic array layout

Linear array, 3 cm spacing, mounted on acoustic head:

[mic0] --- 3cm --- [mic1] --- 3cm --- [mic2] --- 3cm --- [mic3]
                       |
                  [speaker]
                       |
                  [servo pivot]

Power

  • 18650 2-cell pack (7.4V) → L298N motor supply
  • Buck converter (5V/3A) → Raspberry Pi
  • Shared GND between Pi, ESP32, and motor driver
